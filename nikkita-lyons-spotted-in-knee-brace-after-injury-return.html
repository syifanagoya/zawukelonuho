    Nikkita Lyons Spotted In Knee Brace After Injury Return

    WWE has a lot of talented members of their NXT roster, but few have the true upside of Nikkita Lyons. The 24-year-old Superstar is just getting over a knee injury, but it seems that she is still being safe at home.

    10 Jun 2024, 00:00

    WWE has a lot of talented members of their NXT roster, but few have the true upside of Nikkita Lyons. The 24-year-old Superstar is just getting over a knee injury, but it seems that she is still being safe at home.

    Nikkita Lyons is working hard all the time, but her knee injury kept her out of the ring for a year. Now, she is back in the squared circle, and competing on television again, but she is still nursing that knee.

    Lyons was spotted in video on her Instagram, at home, and having a good time with her dog. While she pet her dog with her feet, it was also evident that she was rocking a knee brace still as she made her way through the world.

    Nikkita Lyons is still medically cleared, and we have heard no indication that she injured herself again. She is just living her life, and that requires a knee brace, because she’s done enough to her knee to warrant it at this point.

    Plenty of people have lived their lives with knee braces on, including “Stone Cold” Steve Austin. That might be a good addition to Nikkita Lyons’ character, because she shows no signs of slowing down in WWE.

    We will have to see what is next for Nikkita Lyons. She has a ton of ability, and she is young enough for WWE to turn her into a massive star in the future. Hopefully, she can stay healthy so she can reach her full potential.
