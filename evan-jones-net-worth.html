    What is Evan Jones' Net Worth?

    Evan Jones is an American actor who has a net worth of $2 million. Evan Jones was born in Texas in April 1976. From 2007 to 2008 he starred as Ikey in the television series October Road.

    Jones has had recurring roles as David "Ikey" Eichorn in the TV series Going to California in 2001, Dan in the series Brother & Sisters in 2011, Chris Stratton in the television series Criminal Minds in 2012, Jim Collins in the TV series Houdini in 2014, Colby Moore in the series Graceland in 2015, and Python in the television series CSI: Cyber from 2015 to 2016. Jones has starred in the films Going Greek, 8 Mile, Mr. 3000, The Last Shot, Jarhead, Glory Road, Rescue Dawn, The Book of Ruth, Mirrors 2, The Book of Eli, Answer This!, Gangster Squad, Best Man Down, A Million Ways to Die in the West, Shot Caller, and Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2.
