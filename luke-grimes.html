    The dashing actor was born Luke Timothy Grimes on 21 January 1984 in Dayton, Ohio, the United States. Grimes is the son of a pentecostal pastor, Randy Grimes and Angie Barker. More, he has three siblings. So far, he is a graduate of Dayton Christian High School in 2002. Later after moving to New York City, he studied acting at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts.

    Who Is Luke Grimes Wife?

    The 37-years-old actor is a happily married man. He tied the knot with his wife, Bianca Rodrigues Grimes, an model in 2018. But when it comes to revealing his personal life, he has not shared any glimpse of his married life as he loves to keep it all to himself. 

    Because of his secretive nature, further information regarding their love history is still unknown to the media. But the couple is enjoying their marital relationship privately for two years. And there is no rumor of their separation. Now, they reside in their home town New York City.
