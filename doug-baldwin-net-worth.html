    Doug Baldwin net worth and salary: Doug Baldwin is an American football wide receiver  who has a net worth of $22 million. Doug Baldwin was born in 1988 in Gulf Breeze, Florida. He played youth football in Pensacola, Florida with Alfred Morris of the Washington Redskins. Doug Baldwin played high school football at Gulf Breeze High School. During his senior season, Doug Baldwin had 42 receptions for 682 yards and six touchdowns. Doug Baldwin played college football for the Stanford Cardinal from 2007 to 2010 where he led the Stanford team in receiving yards and touchdowns his senior year.

    Doug Baldwin was an undrafted free agent signed by the Seattle Seahawks at the end of the 2011 NFL lockout to a three year deal worth $1.4 million dollars. Doug Baldwin caught his first NFL touchdown in the first week of the 2011 NFL Season. As a rookie, Baldwin was 4th in reception yardage in the entire NFL and led his team in both receiving yards and receptions. Doug Baldwin was the first undrafted rookie free agent to lead his team in receptions and receiving yards since the AFL-NFL merger in 1970. In 2012, Baldwin was riddled with injuries from dental surgery after diving for a pass to a shoulder injury and a high ankle sprain. In 2013, Doug Baldwin caught five touchdowns, a career high and was second in receptions and yards on the Seattle Seahawks.
