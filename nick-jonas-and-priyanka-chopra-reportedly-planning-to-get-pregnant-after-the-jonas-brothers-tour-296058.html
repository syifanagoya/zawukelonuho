    20 Jul 2024, 00:00

    According to new insider reports, Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra want to wait for him to wrap up his tour with the Jonas Brothers before starting a family together! That way, Nick can dedicate his whole time and attention to being a dad.

    Given the fact that their last stop is on February 22, 2020, the two are still not going to have a kid anytime soon.

    Priyanka and Nick are excited about parenthood but one insider previously told HollywoodLife that they are not in a hurry.

    The newlyweds want to take their time and continue to live their best life just with one another.

    ‘For Priyanka and Nick, family is really important, and they actually want to have a few children. And they want to start soon. They’ll likely work on starting a family next year after the Jonas tour’s completed,’ a new source dished.

    They added that: ‘Nick wants to be there for Priyanka the whole way, so that can only happen when he isn’t on the road. Of course, they will hang out on tour, but Nick would like to have his full attention on Priyanka and be there for their kid as much as possible. When Nick becomes a father, he is going to put his full attention towards that. He cannot wait to start a family with Priyanka, as they talk about it all the time.’

    Nick and Priyanka have only been married for about six months so it makes total sense that they’re not in a hurry to add to the family.
